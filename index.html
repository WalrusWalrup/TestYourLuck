About

Welcome to **Test Your Luck**! This is a fun game where you roll a virtual dice to discover how lucky you really are. Each roll has a chance to give you an item with varying rarities, from **Really Common (1/2)** to the nearly impossible **One in a Million (1/10000)**.

The rarer the result, the harder it is to roll! Are you feeling lucky? Give it a try, and track your results in the inventory section.
